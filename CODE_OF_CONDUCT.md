# Code Of Conduct

Dory is a technical project with a public issue tracker. Keep discussion direct, respectful, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be clear and constructive.
- Assume good faith unless there is clear evidence otherwise.
- Critique code, design, docs, and behavior without personal attacks.
- Respect maintainers' time by keeping issues and PRs scoped.
- Do not post private information, secrets, personal identifiers, or private corpus material.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or targeted personal attacks.
- Publishing another person's private information.
- Repeated off-topic comments after maintainers ask for focus.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues or PRs; block accounts; or report abuse to the hosting platform when needed. If you need to report a conduct problem, contact the maintainers through the repository's private reporting channel when available, or open a minimal issue asking for a private contact path without including sensitive details.
